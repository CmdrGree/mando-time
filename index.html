Mando'a Time System

In Mando'a, we use a different time system.

It is based on there being multiple watches in a day.

The word for "Day" in Mando'a is Tuur.

Tuure are split up into 8 Hakbase, equal to 3 hours each.

Each Hakba has 12 Getu'e, equal to 15 minutes each.

Each Getu has 30 Kumane, equal to half a minute each.

Each Kumana has 30 Kis'lune, equal to a second.
